We focus on blood stem cells and clonal hematopoiesis, how environmental exposures and inflammation shape mutation selection and early steps toward blood cancers (MDS/AML).

Our lab studies how environmental exposures and aging shape blood stem cells and early steps towards blood cancers. We combine genomics, single-cell/spatial profiling, and functional models to map clonal hematopoiesis and uncover targets for prevention and therapy.
