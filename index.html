We study how exposures and aging reshape blood and prostate cancers.

The Divij Verma Lab maps early disease steps in hematologic and prostate cancers. We combine genomics, single-cell and spatial profiling, and computational modeling to understand clonal evolution and uncover targets for prevention and therapy.

Join us

Focus Areas

• Clonal hematopoiesis & early myeloid disease (MDS/AML)
• Prostate cancer evolution, microenvironment & therapy response
• Environmental exposures, aging & inflammation in cancer risk
• Early detection, biomarkers & risk stratification
